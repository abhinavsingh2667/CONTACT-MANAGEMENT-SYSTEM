PROJECT NAME = CONTACT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM.
PROJECT SCOPE :-

1. Purpose

The Contact Management System is designed to store, organize, and manage contact information efficiently. It allows users to add, search, update, and delete contacts from a centralized system.

2. Objectives
Maintain a digital contact directory.
Enable quick searching and retrieval of contact information.
Reduce the need for manual record-keeping.
Improve contact organization and management.

3. Features Included
Add new contacts.
View all contacts.
Search contacts by name, phone number, or email.
Update contact details.
Delete contacts.
Sort contacts alphabetically.
Store multiple details such as name, phone number, email, and address.

4. Users of the System
Personal Users
Store and manage personal contacts.
Quickly access contact details.
Business Users
Maintain customer, client, or employee contact information.
Organize professional contacts efficiently.



5. Benefits
Easy access to contact information.
Saves time in searching contacts.
Reduces data duplication.
Improves data organization.
Simplifies contact management tasks.
